Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet carmelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour;
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast;
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt;
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water;
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus for the pan.

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced;
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar;
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top;
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary);
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries.

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough:

    In a medium sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachement, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
